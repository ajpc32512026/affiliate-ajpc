How To Use Cocoberry Soap

To use Cocoberry soap, first wet your skin, then lather the soap on your palms or a sponge. Gently massage the lather onto your body, and rinse thoroughly. For best results, some recommend leaving the lather on your skin for a few minutes before rinsing. Always perform a patch test and review the ingredients before full application.

  1. Wet your skin: Use lukewarm water to dampen your skin.
  2. Lather the soap: Apply the Cocoberry soap to your palms or a sponge and rub to create a rich lather.
  3. Massage onto your body: Gently massage the lather onto your skin, covering your entire body.
  4. Leave on (optional): For potential micro-peeling benefits, some suggest leaving the lather on for 3-5 minutes before rinsing.
  5. Rinse thoroughly: Rinse off all the soap residue with water.
  6. Follow up: Consider using Cocoberry serum lotion and sunscreen for enhanced results, according to Tess29BeautyProductsShop on Facebook.
  7. Patch test: Before full application, especially the first time, test the soap on a small area of skin to check for any adverse reactions.
  8. Discontinue if needed: If irritation or adverse reaction occurs, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist, as advised by theprettyglam.com.
