How To Use Guava Soap for Acne

To use Guava Soap for acne, wet your skin, apply the soap, lather well, and rinse thoroughly. Gently massage the soap onto your skin in circular motions, focusing on acne-prone areas. For best results, use twice daily. Always do a patch test to check for allergic reactions before full use.

  1. Wet your skin: Start by wetting your face and/or body with lukewarm water.
  2. Apply the soap: Gently massage the Guava Soap onto your skin, creating a lather using your hands or a washcloth.
  3. Lather and massage: Massage the soap in circular motions, focusing on acne-prone areas to exfoliate and cleanse.
  4. Rinse thoroughly: Rinse off all soap residue with lukewarm water.
  5. Dry your skin: Pat your skin dry with a clean, soft towel—avoid rubbing to prevent irritation.
  6. Frequency: Use Guava Soap twice daily, morning and night, for optimal results.
  7. Patch test: Before full use, apply a small amount of lather to a discreet skin area (like under the jaw) and wait 24-48 hours to check for irritation or allergic reactions.
  8. Storage tip: Avoid leaving Guava Soap in standing water to prevent it from becoming mushy. Store it in a dry place or use a draining soap dish.
