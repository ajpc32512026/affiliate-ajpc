How To Use Charcoal Niacinamide Soap

To effectively use Charcoal Niacinamide soap, wet your skin, lather the soap with your hands or a washcloth, gently massage the lather onto your skin, and then rinse thoroughly with water. You can use it daily, both in the morning and evening, focusing on areas that need deep cleansing or have breakouts.

  1. Wet your skin: Use lukewarm or warm water to dampen your skin.
  2. Lather the soap: Rub the soap between your hands or onto a washcloth to create a rich lather.
  3. Apply and massage: Gently massage the lather onto your face and/or body in circular motions.
  4. Rinse thoroughly: Rinse off all the soap with warm water.
  5. Moisturize (optional): After drying, apply a moisturizer to help hydrate your skin.

Additional Tips

  • Frequency: You can use Charcoal Niacinamide soap daily, both morning and night, or adjust based on your skin's needs and how it reacts.
  • Targeted Areas: Focus on areas prone to breakouts or with excess oil and dirt.
  • Skin Sensitivity: If you have sensitive skin, consider using it less frequently or diluting the lather with water.
  • Storage: Keep the soap in a dry place to prevent it from dissolving quickly.
