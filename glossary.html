Skincare Glossary

Explore the natural active ingredients powering our curated beauty selections.

Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid)

A powerful antioxidant that brightens dull skin tones, fades hyperpigmentation, and promotes collagen synthesis for a firmer, more radiant complexion.

Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)

An exceptionally versatile active ingredient that strengthens the skin barrier, regulates excess oil production, minimizes pore appearance, and soothes redness.

Hyaluronic Acid

A naturally occurring humectant capable of holding up to 1,000 times its weight in water, drawing deep moisture into the skin to plump, smooth, and hydrate.

Kojic Acid

A natural byproduct of fermented rice, renowned in traditional skincare for its gentle exfoliating properties and its ability to visibly lighten sun spots and age spots.

Aloe Vera

A soothing, moisture-rich plant botanical loaded with vitamins and minerals that rapidly cools irritated skin, accelerates healing, and softens rough dry patches.

Collagen

An essential structural protein that provides the building blocks for skin firmness and elasticity, helping to visibly minimize fine lines and maintain suppleness.

Activated Charcoal

A natural purifying agent that acts like a magnet to extract deep-seated dirt, excess sebum, and environmental impurities from pores for a balanced, clear skin feel.

Guava Extract

Packed with antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties, this natural extract is highly effective in clearing blemish-prone skin and soothing acne-related irritation.

Sunflower Oil

A lightweight, non-comedogenic botanical oil packed with Vitamin E that deeply nourishes dry tissue, locks in natural moisture, and shields skin from environmental stressors.
