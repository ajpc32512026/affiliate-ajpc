Welcome to A&J

We're more than just a collection — we're a story in every slice and every bar. A&J started in two places at once — one of us in Australia, the other in the Philippines — with a long-distance love strong enough to cross oceans, time zones, and dodgy internet connections. What began as recipe swaps and care packages has grown into something real. Something we can build — together.

Our food is homemade, handwritten, and honestly earned — the kind you bake at midnight just to feel close, or recreate from memory because it reminds you of home.

Our soaps and skincare are crafted in the Philippines, made by local hands who know their craft — using natural ingredients, traditional techniques, and no shortcuts. Ana personally selects every item we offer, only choosing products she trusts, uses, and stands behind. If it doesn’t meet her standards, it doesn’t make the cut.

Every slice. Every bar.
Built with love. Made with purpose.
And now, shared with you.
