1. Made by Hand, Not by Robots

All of our products are handcrafted in small batches in the Philippines. Variations in shape, color, or scent are completely normal — they’re the personal touch of the local makers, not flaws.

We don’t make them ourselves — we work with trusted creators to bring these beautiful products to you.

2. Pricing & Payment

All prices are listed in Philippine Pesos (₱). We accept GCash and credit/debit cards via Stripe. Orders are processed only after full payment is confirmed.

3. Delivery & Pickup

We proudly deliver anywhere across the Philippines — from Luzon to Mindanao, your handcrafted treasures are on their way. Delivery fees vary based on your region and the weight of your order. Exact charges will be communicated before dispatch, so you’ll know what to expect—no hidden fees, no surprises. Payment for delivery must be completed via GCash prior to shipment.

For those nearby, pickup remains free and convenient. Just give us a shout!
