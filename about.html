Our Story

A&J Personal Collections started as a simple way for us to share our favorite skincare and beauty finds. Since settling in Australia, we have expanded our focus to include everyday fashion and practical homewares. Today, we curate and recommend a variety of items we use in our daily lives—ranging from clothing for the whole family to kitchen and home essentials.

We run this platform together as a team. Anna focuses on style, design, and visual appeal, while John looks at the practical side—checking for functionality, durability, and reasonable pricing. This balance helps us make sure that every recommendation is both nice to look at and genuinely useful.

Our rule is straightforward: we only feature products we would actually use ourselves. If we wouldn't wear a garment, cook with a tool, or have it in our own home, it doesn't make the list.

Based in Australia with roots in the Philippines, we appreciate simple, practical design. Whether we are sharing home finds, fashion, or skincare, our goal is simply to help you find reliable, budget-friendly essentials that make daily life a little easier.
