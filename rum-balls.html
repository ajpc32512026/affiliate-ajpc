Chocolate Rum Balls

Mix & Roll Bliss

Simple, nostalgic, and deliciously boozy — these Chocolate Rum Balls are the perfect no-bake treat for grown-ups who still believe in sprinkles and a good stiff drink.

🍬 Components

  • 4 cups (400g) cake crumbs
  • ½ cup cocoa
  • ¼ cup apricot jam (warmed)
  • 1 cup dark rum (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp water
  • Chocolate sprinkles

📝 Method

  1. Combine cake crumbs, cocoa, jam, rum, and water in a large bowl.
  2. Mix thoroughly until evenly combined.
  3. Roll into balls about 2 teaspoons in size.
  4. Coat each ball generously in chocolate sprinkles.
  5. Chill for 2 hours before serving — if you can wait that long.

🍫 Note

Makes about 45 bite-sized rum balls — perfect for sharing (or not).
