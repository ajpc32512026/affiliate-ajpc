Hummingbird

A tropical, nostalgic bake kissed with bananas and pineapple.

A slice of the tropics baked with love — sweet bananas, golden pineapple, and a whisper of spice.

🍳 Yield:

1 x 9-inch round cake

⏱ Time:

15 min prep · 25–30 min bake · 45 min total

📝 Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 1½ cups plain flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Wet Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple (with juice, not drained)

👩‍🍳 Method — Let’s Bake

  1. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cinnamon, baking soda, and baking powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the eggs, mashed bananas, and crushed pineapple.
  4. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined. Don’t overmix — the magic lies in a light hand.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  6. Bake for 25–30 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Slice, serve, and savour — preferably with someone who appreciates a good old-fashioned bake.
